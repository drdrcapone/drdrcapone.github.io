Resources for Behavioral, Mental, and Interpersonal Health:

Mental Health Lines:

  • Colorado 988 - Phone: 988

Colorado's 988 Phone line service connects callers in need of any kind of support for mental health, emotional, or substance use crisis and is inclusive of all people.

  • Colorado Crisis Services - Phone: 844-493-TALK (8255) and SMS: 38255 (Text "TALK")

Colorado Crisis Services is open 24/7 to discuss any mental health, substance use, or emotional concerns.

  • Open Hearts Advocates 24-Hour Crisis Line - Phone: 970-824-2400

Open Hearts Advocates is a 24/7 service available for free and confidential support over the phone.
